3. Service Description

We provide dedicated L2 blockchain infrastructure including:

  • Block explorer access
  • Multi-region redundancy for high availability
  • Full EVM compatibility

Credits are debited monthly to cover the cost of operating your chain infrastructure. The monthly rate depends on your chain's activity level and resource usage.

4. Balance Depletion

  • Low balance warning. We will display a warning in your dashboard when your credit balance falls below two months of estimated usage.
  • Zero balance. When your credit balance reaches zero, your L2 chain will stop producing finalized blocks. This is a natural consequence of the infrastructure running out of operational funding, not a punitive action.
  • No data loss. Your chain data is preserved on disk even when the chain is stalled. No data is deleted upon balance depletion.
  • Resumption. You may resume your chain at any time by purchasing additional credits. Your chain will resume block production automatically once credits are available.

5. Inactivity and Data Retention

  • 90-day archive. If your credit balance has been zero for 90 consecutive days with no account activity, we may archive your chain data to cold storage and reclaim the server resources.
  • Restoration. You may request restoration of archived chain data by purchasing credits sufficient to cover at least one month of hosting. Restoration may take up to 48 hours.
  • 1-year deletion. If your chain data has been archived for one year with no payment or restoration request, we reserve the right to permanently delete the archived data. This action is irreversible.
